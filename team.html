ABOUT

QUEER LATIN DANCE SD

Creating spaces where everyone can dance authentically, celebrating both the art of salsa and the beauty of our community.

Building connections through dance, music, and shared joy. Every step tells our story.

From the studio to the social, we're creating moments that matter and memories that last.

Together we dance, together we grow. Honoring the roots of Latin dance while creating our own legacy.

MEET THE TEAM

Michelle Fernandez (she/they)

Founder of Queer Latin Dance SD and organizer of Queer Salsa Social Events. As a lifelong performer, her background ranges from experiences in competitive gymnastics to singing with Luciano Pavarotti. She fell in love with salsa during college as a creative escape from her engineering studies, an exploration that allowed her to perform with the salsa and bachata group Sokaliente. After meeting her partner Pat and coming out shortly thereafter, Michelle decided to introduce Pat to the world of Latin dance, however she faced some push back when she wanted to learn how to lead. This experience highlighted the lack of inclusive dance spaces and motivated her to set out to build an inclusive, affirming Latin dance space for the queer community in San Diego.

Pat Petel (she/they)

Co-organizer of Queer Latin Dance SD, has been actively involved in its creation alongside her partner, Michelle. She contributes her skills in photography, web development, event coordination, and overall project management to help ensure QLDSD events run successfully.
